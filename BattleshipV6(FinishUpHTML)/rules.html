Rules

Objective

Sink your opponent's fleet of five ships before he sinks yours.

Prepare for Battle!

Players will place five of their ships on their grid
either horizontally or vertically, not diagonally.

How to Play

Decide who will go first. Then alternate turns,
shooting at a coordinate on the opponent's grid.
Once a shot is fired, a coordinate without a ship is
a miss, while a coordinate with a ship is a hit.

It's a Hit!

Once a ship is hit, it will be marked on
your map as a hit marker and the player
will be allowed to shoot a second time.

It's a Miss!

Once a ship is missed, it will be marked
on your map a missed marker and
the your opponent will go next.

Sinking a Ship

Once a ship has been hit on all of its markers,
it will be announced that a ship has sunk.

Winning The Game

To win the game, one player needs to sink
their opponent's fleet of five ships
before all of their ships sink.
